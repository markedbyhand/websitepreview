// How a custom order works

Three steps from idea to doormat

No three-day email chains. Each step has its own tool — use them in order, or jump straight to a quote.

STEP 01

Preview

Build the design and see exactly how it'll look — a name in edge-lit acrylic, or your text laid over the real product.

STEP 02

Price

Drop your DXF into the quote engine. It measures size, cut length and engrave area, then prices it from a live rate card.

Order

Happy with the preview and price? Confirm the order. We cut it, check it by eye, photograph it and post it tracked.

// Who's behind it

Two people, one laser, a lot of care

Marked By Hand is a husband-and-wife studio run out of a home workshop in Sydney. Tim runs the design and the CO₂ laser; Kannika handles styling, photography and getting your order packed and posted.

Because it's just us, every piece gets looked at by a human before it goes in the box. If something isn't right, it doesn't ship.
