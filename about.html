// Who we are

A small Sydney workshop that takes the word "handmade" literally.

Marked By Hand is just the two of us — Tim and Kannika — a CO₂ laser, and a spare room that's slowly been taken over by offcuts of acrylic and bamboo.

How it started

It began with one personalised gift that turned into ten, then a hundred. We're makers and tinkerers at heart, so instead of fighting with slow quoting and clunky proofs, we built our own tools — the instant quote engine, the night-light builder, the engraving previewer you'll find on this site.

How we work

Tim handles the design files and runs the laser. Kannika styles and photographs every piece and packs your order for post. Because it's a two-person shop, nothing leaves without a human looking at it first.

We cut and engrave on a 60W CO₂ laser from our home in Sydney. Most orders go out within two to four days, posted tracked across Australia.

What we care about

  • Getting it right. If a cut's off or an engrave isn't crisp, we redo it.
  • Showing you first. Preview the design and see the price before you commit.
  • Honest materials. Real bamboo, cast acrylic, natural slate — no surprises.
