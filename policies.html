Shipping

We post Australia-wide with tracking. Most orders are made and dispatched within 2–4 business days; you'll get a tracking number by email when it ships.

Rates

Postage is calculated at checkout by size and destination. Smaller items ship as a tracked large letter where they fit, to keep costs down.

Pickup

Local pickup in Sydney can be arranged for some orders — get in touch before ordering.

Returns & remakes

Because most items are personalised and made to order, we can't accept change-of-mind returns on custom pieces.

If something's wrong

If your order arrives damaged, faulty, or different to what was confirmed, contact us within 7 days with a photo and we'll remake or refund it. Our mistakes are on us.

Proofs

For custom files we send a proof before cutting. Once you approve a proof, that's what we make — so give names, spelling and dates a careful check.

Privacy

We collect only what we need to make and post your order — your name, contact details, shipping address and order notes.

We don't sell your information. Payment is handled by our checkout provider; we never see your full card details. Order data is kept only as long as needed for records and tax.

Want your details removed after an order's complete? Just ask.

Terms of sale

Placing an order is an offer to buy, which we accept when we confirm and begin making it. Prices are in AUD and include GST where applicable.

Custom and personalised items are made to your supplied details and approved proof. We're not able to remake at our cost for errors in details you supplied and approved.

Designs we create for a custom job remain our work; you're free to use the finished product as you like.
